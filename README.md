# 🚴‍♂️ Cyclistic Bike-Share Analysis
### Google Data Analytics Professional Certificate Capstone Case Study

## 📌 Introduction & Case Background
Cyclistic is a successful bike-share program in Chicago. This project analyzes how annual members and casual riders use the service differently to guide future marketing strategies.

---

## 📑 The 6 Phases of the Data Analysis Process

### 1. Ask
The core business goal is to convert casual riders into annual members by identifying behavioral differences.

### 2. Prepare
Data consists of historical bike trip records. Privacy is maintained as PII is removed. Note: The raw data file (114MB) is excluded from this repository due to size limits.

### 3. Process
* **Tools:** SQL (BigQuery) & R.
* **Cleaning:** Removed duplicates, handled missing values, and calculated `ride_length` and `day_of_week`.

### 4. Analyze & Share
To ensure data integrity, the analysis was performed in **Power BI** and **R**, yielding identical results.

#### Key Trends:
* **Weekly Patterns:** Annual members commute on weekdays; Casual riders peak on weekends.
* **Duration:** Casual riders travel for significantly longer durations.

---

### 5. Act
1. **Targeted Weekend Memberships:** Focus on weekend-specific passes.
2. **Value Campaigns:** Show casual riders the long-term savings of an annual membership.
3. **Digital Marketing:** Deploy QR codes at recreational stations during peak weekend hours.
